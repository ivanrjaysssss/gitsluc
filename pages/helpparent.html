Parent - Help Page
How to:
  • View Grades?

    1. On your Home Page, there is an input box where you should type the ID Number of the Student.

    2. Click the button below it. You will be redirected to another page where you will see first if the student you were looking for is correct.

    3. Choose the school year of the grades you wish to view then click "Proceed"

  • Change your Username, Password,
    ID Number?

    1. Click the bar icon on the upper right corner of your screen, select "Settings".

    2. Replace the profile info you wish to change then click "Save".
