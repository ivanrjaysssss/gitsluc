Teacher - Help Page
How to:
  • Create a Subject?

    1. On your Home Page, click the circular button on the bottom right corner of your screen.

    2. Select the school year, department, course, year, section and subject title of the subject.

    3. Click the "Create Subject" button below.

  • Delete a Subject?

    1. On your Home Page, swipe the subject title of your chosen subject to left then click "Delete".

    2. Click on OK if you want to confirm the deletion of the subject. All the data from the subject(Student and Grades) will be permanently deleted.

  • Delete a Student Account?

    1. Click the bar icon on the upper right corner of your screen, select "Delete Student/s Account".

    2. Search the name of the student that you want to delete.

    3. Select the name, then a confirmation window that will appear.

    4. Click on OK if you want to confirm the deletion of the student's account.

  • Enroll a Student to a Subject?

    1. On your Home Page, select the subject where you want to enroll the student. A series of buttons will appear.

    2. Click on the "Add Students to Subject" button.

    3. Search for the name or year and section of the student that you want to enroll, swipe to right then click the "Add" button.

    4. Click on OK if you want to confirm the enrollment of the student in the subject.

  • Remove a Student from a Subject?

    1. On your Home Page, select the subject where you want to remove the student. A series of buttons will appear.

    2. Click on the "Remove Students from Subject" button.

    3. Search for the name or year and section of the student that you want to remove, select the name.

    4. Click on OK if you want to confirm on removing the student in the subject.

  • Enroll Multiple Students to a Subject?

    1. On your Home Page, select the subject where you want to enroll the students. A series of buttons will appear.

    2. Click on the "Add Students by Categories" button.

    3. Choose the department, course, year and section of the students that you want to enroll in the subject.

    4. Click the button below if you are sure with the categories that you've chosen in order to enroll the students.

  • Edit or Input Grades of a Student?

    1. On your Home Page, select the subject where you want to edit/input the grades of a student. A series of buttons will appear.

    2. Click on the "Show Enrolled Students" button.

    3. Search for the name or year and section of the student that you want to input/edit his/her grade.

    4. Swipe to right then click on "Edit Grade" button.

    5. Input his/her midterm and final grade then choose the type of transmutation grade to be applied on the overall grade of the student on the subject.

    6. Click "Save" to save the grades that you've submitted.

  • Show the Grades of Student/s as
    Incomplete(INC)?

    1. On your Home Page, select the subject where you want to change the display of grades of a student. A series of buttons will appear.

    2. Click on the "Show Enrolled Students" button.

    3. Search for the name or year and section of the student that you want to change the display of the student's grade.

    4. Swipe to right then click on "INC" button.

    5. Choose to show the grade as Incomplete.

    6. Click "Save" to save the changes.

  • Change your Username, Password,
    ID Number?

    1. Click the bar icon on the upper right corner of your screen, select "Settings".

    2. Replace the profile info you wish to change then click "Save".
